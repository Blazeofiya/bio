My Journey

As a child, I watched my father devote himself to work—even on weekends—in pursuit of a better life for our family. His steadfast resolve amid personal challenges taught me to embrace forgiveness, appreciate small blessings, and seek balance. Whether on camping trips, gathered around a campfire, or simply tossing a football, he showed me that life isn’t solely about the daily grind—it’s also about nurturing close relationships, growing as individuals, and living with deliberate purpose.

After earning a business degree from the University of Central Florida and spending a transformative year in Barcelona—where I embraced a Mediterranean culture that champions balanced living—I returned to the fast-paced corporate world as an insurance underwriter. There, I discovered that small, thoughtful changes could dramatically improve efficiency and fulfillment.

A Turning Point

My corporate journey wasn’t without its challenges. Facing the bureaucratic hurdles of a large organization and coping with my father’s Parkinson’s diagnosis in early 2024 forced me to reassess my priorities. I chose to step away from my job, dedicating time to family and personal well-being. During this period, I rediscovered the joy of hands-on learning—whether it was making soap, learning to cut my own hair, tending a garden, playing basketball, or even exploring Zen philosophies during a month-long stay in Japan.

My Philosophy & How I Can Help You

I believe that true success stems from a balanced, intentional life. Leveraging the vast resources of AI and modern technology, I help you learn new skills and implement small, transformative changes every day. My journey—from the corporate world to a life of proactive health and efficiency—fuels my passion for guiding you toward a lifestyle where productivity and presence go hand in hand.

Whether you’re seeking to enhance personal well-being, boost workplace productivity, or optimize business operations, my personalized, hands-on approach cuts through the noise and overthinking. Together, we’ll focus on strategies that allow you to invest more in family, health, and the pursuits that truly matter.

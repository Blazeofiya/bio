My Journey

Growing up, I watched my father work tirelessly—often seven days a week—driven by the desire to provide a positive future for our family. Despite the challenges we faced, his commitment instilled in me values of forgiveness, gratitude, and the importance of balance. Whether we were camping, building fires, or simply tossing a football at the park, he taught me that life is about more than just work—it’s about connection, growth, and living with intention.

After earning a business degree from the University of Central Florida and spending a transformative year in Barcelona—where I embraced a culture that champions work-life balance—I returned to the fast-paced corporate world. I dabbled in software sales and insurance underwriting, where I quickly learned that efficiency could be improved with small, thoughtful changes. I became passionate about automating mundane tasks and streamlining processes to make work simpler and more fulfilling.

A Turning Point

However, my corporate journey wasn’t without its challenges. Feeling a lack of direction, and when my father was diagnosed with Parkinson’s in early 2024, I was forced to reevaluate my life priorities. I stepped away from my demanding job, choosing instead to spend time with my family and focus on my own well-being. During this period, I rediscovered the joy of hands-on learning—making soap, tending a garden, playing basketball, and even exploring zen philosophies during a month-long stay in Tokyo.

My Philosophy & How I Can Help You

These experiences taught me that true success comes from living a balanced, intentional life. With the vast resources available through AI and modern technology, I believe that anyone can learn new skills and make small, transformative changes every day. My journey—from a hectic corporate environment to a life of proactive health and efficiency—drives my passion for helping you design a life where you can be both productive and present.

Whether you’re looking to improve your personal well-being, boost productivity at work, or optimize your business operations, I’m here to provide a personalized, hands-on approach. I help you cut through the noise, limit overthinking, and focus on strategies that let you spend more time with family, invest in your health, and ultimately, live the life you envision.

Let’s Connect

If you’re ready to reclaim control over your time and energy, and to focus on what truly matters, let’s start a conversation. I’m here to help you make meaningful, lasting changes—one small step at a time.

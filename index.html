My Journey

As a child, I watched my father devote himself to work, even on weekends, for the betterment of our family. His resolve amid personal struggles taught me to embrace the uncomfortable, appreciate small blessings, and seek balance. Whether on road trips or simply tossing a football, he showed me that life isn’t solely about the daily grind—it’s also about nurturing close relationships, growing as individuals, and living with deliberate purpose.

After earning a business degree from the University of Central Florida and spending a transformative year in Barcelona—where I embraced a Mediterranean culture that champions balanced living—I returned to the fast-paced corporate world as an insurance underwriter. There, I discovered that small, thoughtful changes could dramatically improve efficiency and fulfillment.

A Turning Point

Facing the bureaucratic hurdles of a large organization helped me reassess my priorities. I chose to step away from my job, dedicating time to family and personal well-being. During this period, I rediscovered the joy of hands-on learning—whether it was making soap, tending a garden, playing basketball, or even exploring Zen philosophies during a month-long stay in Japan. Now I've reapplied my skills in a growing startup called Vali Cyber.

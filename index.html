My Journey

Despite the challenges we faced, his commitment instilled in me values of forgiveness, gratitude, and the importance of balance. Whether we were camping, building fires, or simply tossing a football at the park, he taught me that life is about more than just work—it’s about connection, growth, and living with intention.

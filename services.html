One-on-One Coaching Services

Right now, I’m focusing on personalized, one-on-one sessions to help you reclaim control over your life. Each session is typically one hour long and is structured into three segments: a brief consultation to understand your goals, a focused coaching period tailored to your needs, and a wrap-up to review progress and plan next steps. My approach blends physical activity, strategic budgeting, and habit transformation— all tailored to your unique lifestyle and goals. As I build a client base, I plan to expand into group sessions and workshops.

1. Personalized Lifestyle & Fitness Coaching

What It Is: Tailor-made fitness routines that match your lifestyle. Whether you’re into basketball, pickleball, or beach workouts, we’ll work together outdoors to design an active, balanced plan.

Where We Meet: Local parks, the beach, or any outdoor space that inspires you.

Session Details:

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Structure: Start with a quick consultation, engage in a personalized workout plan, and conclude with tips for ongoing progress.

Recommended Donation: $40 per hour (Pay what you can—this is simply a guideline.)

2. Habit & Efficiency Transformation Coaching

What It Is: Identify and eliminate counterproductive habits and inefficiencies in your daily routine. Drawing from my corporate background and hands-on experiences, I’ll help you streamline your life so you can focus on what’s truly essential.

Session Details:

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Structure: Begin with goal-setting and assessment, work through targeted habit transformation strategies, and wrap up with actionable next steps.

Recommended Donation: $35 per hour (Your contribution helps keep these transformative sessions accessible to everyone.)

3. Budgeting & Money-Saving Advice

What It Is: Learn practical strategies to cut unnecessary expenses, grow your savings, and optimize your personal finances. I offer insights that cut through conventional advice, helping you achieve lasting financial stability and practice intentional spending.

Session Details:

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Structure: Start with an evaluation of your current financial habits, explore customized budgeting techniques, and end with a clear plan to enhance your savings.

Recommended Donation: $35 per hour (Flexible contributions ensure that anyone can benefit from this guidance.)

4. Combined Coaching Sessions

What It Is: For those looking for a holistic approach, these sessions blend physical activity coaching (like basketball or beach fitness) with strategic planning on habits and budgeting.

Session Details:

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Structure: A seamless integration of active coaching and strategic planning, ensuring you get a comprehensive, impactful session in one hour.

Recommended Donation: $45 per hour (A combined session that maximizes impact in a single hour.)

5. Digital Transformation & AI Insights Coaching

What It Is: Harness data and digital technology to transform your strategy—whether you're pursuing personal growth, business innovation, or everyday efficiency. In these sessions, we'll use advanced AI and data analytics to uncover financial trends, pinpoint consumer friction points, and reveal operational inefficiencies. We'll also conduct competitive analyses, build custom websites, and integrate smart automation solutions to streamline your workflow and daily life.

Session Details:

  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Structure: Begin with an overview of your current digital practices, dive into data-driven strategies, and conclude with actionable insights and next steps.

Recommended Donation: $50 per hour (Invest in your digital future—these sessions equip you with the skills to excel in a data-driven world.)

The Donation Philosophy

I believe that everyone should have access to transformational guidance regardless of their financial situation. The recommended donation amounts are simply suggestions—they’re meant to honor the value of the time and expertise shared, while remaining flexible:

  • Contribute More: If you’re able, your extra contribution helps me expand services for the community.
  • Contribute Less: If your budget is tight, you’re still welcome to participate and benefit from these sessions.

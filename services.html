One-on-One Coaching Services

Right now, I’m focusing on personalized, one-on-one sessions to help you reclaim control over your life. My approach blends physical activity, strategic budgeting, and habit transformation—all tailored to your unique lifestyle and goals. As I build a client base, I plan to expand into group sessions and workshops.

1. Personalized Lifestyle & Fitness Coaching

What It Is: Tailor-made fitness routines that match your lifestyle. Whether you’re into basketball, pickleball, or beach workouts, we’ll work together outdoors to design an active, balanced plan.

Where We Meet: Local parks, the beach, or any outdoor space that inspires you.

Recommended Donation: $40 per hour (Pay what you can—this is simply a guideline.)

2. Habit & Efficiency Transformation Coaching

What It Is: Identify and eliminate bad habits and inefficiencies in your daily routine. Drawing from my corporate background and hands-on experiences, I’ll help you streamline your life so you can focus on what truly matters.

Recommended Donation: $35 per hour (Your contribution helps keep these transformative sessions accessible to everyone.)

3. Budgeting & Money-Saving Advice

What It Is: Learn practical strategies to cut unnecessary expenses, build sustainable savings, and optimize your personal finances. I offer insights honed from years of navigating and improving complex processes.

Recommended Donation: $35 per hour (Flexible contributions ensure that anyone can benefit from this guidance.)

4. Combined Coaching Sessions

What It Is: For those looking for a holistic approach, these sessions blend physical activity coaching (like basketball or beach fitness) with strategic planning on habits and budgeting.

Recommended Donation: $45 per hour (A combined session that maximizes impact in a single hour.)

The Donation Philosophy

I believe that everyone should have access to transformational guidance regardless of their financial situation. The recommended donation amounts are simply suggestions—they’re meant to honor the value of the time and expertise shared, while remaining flexible:

  • Contribute More: If you’re able, your extra contribution helps me expand services for the community.
  • Contribute Less: If your budget is tight, you’re still welcome to participate and benefit from these sessions.

Let’s Connect

If you’re ready to start your journey towards a balanced, intentional, and efficient life—one small step at a time—get in touch to schedule your one-on-one session. Together, we can cut through the noise and focus on what truly matters.
